Remote Patient Monitoring

The Future of Actionable Data

Remote patient monitoring is an essential tool for care facilities, enabling them to seamlessly track and monitor residents' health data through Total Remote Care's advanced mobile medical services and technology.

  • Daily Vitals Collection
    Care providers collect vitals on-site and send them to the facility's EHR system for real-time monitoring.
  • Consistent Tracking
    Vitals collected consistently for a more accurate picture of patient health over time.
  • Data-Driven Decisions
    Use RPM data to make informed decisions and prioritize care for patients who need it most.
  • Reduced Complications
    Early detection of health issues reduces risk of complications and emergency care needs.

Chronic Care Management

Empowering Care Through Data-Driven Insights

Chronic care management enables long-term care facilities to seamlessly track and monitor residents' health data, identifying potential issues early and ensuring a higher standard of care.

  • Continuous Coordinated Care
    Leveraging a team of healthcare professionals including physicians, nurses, and specialists.
