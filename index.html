About Us

Walk into any long-term care facility at 3 a.m. and you'll likely find dedicated nurses moving from room to room, documenting by hand, and wondering, "What am I missing?"

We saw skilled professionals spending more time on paperwork than patients, early warning signs slipping through the cracks, and data scattered on clipboards only to become actionable far too late.

Total Remote Care exists to change that. Our technology continuously monitors, intelligently analyzes and proactively alerts — empowering your team with insights when they matter most. We free your nurses to focus on what counts: delivering more attentive, more meaningful care.

Remote Patient Monitoring
Catch Problems Before They Happen

Think of RPM as your facility's early warning system. Instead of waiting for a crisis at 2 AM, our continuous monitoring spots the subtle changes that signal trouble ahead. Your nurses get real-time alerts about declining vitals, giving them the jump on interventions that keep residents healthy, stable, and thriving—not bouncing between the ER and your facility.

Better Outcomes: Early intervention means fewer complications, faster recovery, and residents who spend more time living well instead of fighting off preventable emergencies.

Around-the-Clock Monitoring

Vitals tracked 24/7 without disturbing residents during sleep or activities—just quiet, continuous oversight that catches changes early.

Smart Alerts, Not Noise

Get notified when it matters. Our system learns baselines and only flags meaningful deviations, so your team can act fast on real issues.

Auto-Documentation

Every reading flows straight into your EHR—no manual entry, no clipboards, no chasing down data at shift change.

Trend Spotting

See patterns emerge over days and weeks. Catch gradual decline before it becomes rapid deterioration that impacts quality of life.

Prevent Hospital Transfers

Chronic Care Management
Keep Complex Cases on Track

When residents are juggling diabetes, heart failure, COPD, and more, it's easy for something to slip through the cracks. CCM brings order to the chaos with structured monthly check-ins, coordinated care plans, and proactive medication management. We help your team stay ahead of exacerbations before they land someone in the hospital—keeping residents healthier, more stable, and living with better quality of life.

Better Outcomes: Coordinated care means fewer ER visits, better symptom management, and residents who maintain independence longer—that's what success looks like.

Principal Care Management & Advanced PCM
Precision Care for High-Risk Residents

Some residents need more than standard monitoring—they need laser-focused attention on their primary condition. PCM zeroes in on that single complex diagnosis, while Advanced PCM takes it to the next level with AI-powered predictions and behavioral tracking. Both are designed to spot trouble brewing and give your team time to act before things go sideways—ultimately delivering better health outcomes and preventing unnecessary decline.

Better Outcomes: Precision monitoring means catching issues at the first sign, leading to faster interventions, fewer complications, and residents who maintain their functional independence longer.
