Advancing Post-Acute Care Through Clinical Innovation

We founded Total Remote Care after witnessing firsthand the limitations of traditional vital sign monitoring in long-term care settings. Skilled clinicians were making critical decisions based on infrequent spot-checks and retrospective data—missing the early physiological warnings that could prevent adverse events.

Our platform addresses this fundamental gap by providing continuous, non-invasive monitoring that captures the complete clinical picture. We don't just collect data—we transform it into actionable clinical intelligence that supports evidence-based decision-making at the point of care.

Today, we partner with skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, and memory care units across Florida to deliver measurably better resident outcomes while supporting the clinical teams who provide care every day.

Our Clinical Philosophy

The best interventions happen before symptoms become critical. Our approach is built on three fundamental principles.

Continuous Awareness Beats Periodic Assessment

Traditional vital sign checks every 4-8 hours miss the critical changes that happen in between. Continuous monitoring reveals the complete physiological story—the gradual oxygen desaturation at 3 AM, the subtle heart rate elevation that signals early sepsis, the respiratory pattern that indicates impending decompensation. When you see everything, you miss nothing.

Intelligence Without Intervention Burden

The best monitoring system is the one residents don't notice. No wearables to charge, no adhesives to irritate skin, no devices to lose or break. Our contactless sensors work silently in the background while residents sleep, socialize, and live their lives. Your staff gets the insights without the implementation headaches.

Data Becomes Wisdom Through Context

Raw numbers don't save lives—clinical judgment does. Our AI doesn't replace your nurses' expertise; it amplifies it. By learning each resident's unique baseline and identifying meaningful deviations, we filter out the noise and surface the signals that matter. You get fewer alerts, but every one deserves attention.
