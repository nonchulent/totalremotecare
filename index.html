Our Core Solutions

Comprehensive services designed to transform care delivery and unlock new revenue streams.

📱

Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)

Continuous collection of physiological data using cellular‑enabled devices that transmit instantly without Wi‑Fi, hubs, or pairing.

  • Real‑time vitals (BP, glucose, weight, SpO₂)
  • Automatic transmission via private cellular network
  • CPT 99453, 99454, 99457, 99458
  • Predictive analytics for early intervention
  • 24/7 monitoring capabilities
🏥

Chronic Care Management (CCM)

Comprehensive non‑face‑to‑face management for residents with 2+ chronic conditions, fully reimbursable by CMS.

  • Monthly care plan development and monitoring
  • Daily risk reports prioritizing at‑risk residents
  • Medication reconciliation and management
  • CPT 99490, 99439, 99487
  • Care coordination across providers
🔮

Ambient Sensing Technology

Contactless monitoring using advanced radar and AI technology for continuous, dignity‑preserving care.

  • No wearables or resident interaction required
  • Fall prediction 30–60 seconds before occurrence
  • Continuous vital signs without contact
  • Sleep pattern and activity monitoring
  • Privacy‑preserving (no cameras/mics)
