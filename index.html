Common Questions

Learn more about how remote monitoring works in your facility

How long does installation take?
Most installations are completed within 3-5 business days, depending on facility size. We schedule around your operations and can work in phases if needed. Staff training happens concurrently so your team is ready when the system goes live.
Does this integrate with our existing EHR system?
Yes. We work with most major long-term care EHR systems including PointClickCare, MatrixCare, and others. Data flows directly into your existing charts without duplicate entry. We handle the technical integration during setup.
What happens if the equipment malfunctions?
We provide 24/7 technical support and keep backup equipment on hand. In most cases, we can troubleshoot remotely or have a technician on-site within hours. All equipment comes with our warranty and maintenance is included in your service agreement.
How do we ensure resident privacy?
All our systems are HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption. We conduct regular security audits and staff training on privacy protocols. Only authorized personnel have access to resident data, and all access is logged for compliance purposes.
What's included in the monthly service fee?
Your monthly fee covers all equipment, software access, cloud storage, 24/7 technical support, system updates, routine maintenance, and replacement of any faulty equipment. There are no hidden fees or per-resident charges.
