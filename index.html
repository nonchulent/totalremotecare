About Total Remote Care

Transforming long-term care through intelligent, proactive monitoring

Our Mission

Walk into any long-term care facility at 3 a.m. and you'll likely find dedicated nurses moving from room to room, documenting by hand, and wondering, "What am I missing?"

We saw skilled professionals spending more time on paperwork than patients, early warning signs slipping through the cracks, and data scattered on clipboards only to become actionable far too late.

Total Remote Care exists to change that. Our technology continuously monitors, intelligently analyzes and proactively alerts—empowering your team with insights when they matter most.

What Drives Us

Every day, nurses juggle impossible demands: monitoring dozens of residents, documenting every detail, responding to emergencies, and trying to provide compassionate care. It's not sustainable, and residents pay the price when warning signs go unnoticed.

We believe technology should empower caregivers, not burden them. Our ambient monitoring platform works silently in the background, catching the subtle changes that human eyes might miss during a busy shift.

When vitals trend downward, when breathing patterns change, when movement decreases—our AI alerts your team immediately, giving them the precious time needed to intervene before a minor issue becomes an emergency.

The Result

Nurses who can focus on care instead of documentation. Residents who stay healthier and more stable. Families who have peace of mind knowing their loved ones are continuously monitored. And facilities that see measurable improvements in outcomes, efficiency, and satisfaction.

That's the future of long-term care. That's Total Remote Care.

RPM

Remote Patient Monitoring

Your facility's early warning system. Continuous monitoring spots subtle changes that signal trouble ahead, giving your nurses real-time alerts about declining vitals before crisis hits.

  • 24/7 vital sign tracking without disturbing residents
  • Smart alerts that flag meaningful deviations
  • Automated documentation flowing to your EHR

CCM

Chronic Care Management

When residents juggle multiple conditions, CCM brings order to chaos. Structured check-ins, coordinated care plans, and proactive medication management keep complex cases on track.

  • Personalized care plans for multiple conditions
  • Scheduled monthly touchpoints as safety net
  • Medication tracking and interaction monitoring
  • Full care team collaboration and coordination
  • Better symptom control and quality of life

Our Technology

Ambient sensors, AI intelligence, and seamless integration

Ambient Sensors. Continuous Awareness. Zero Burden.

Our discreet sensors capture micro-movements and cardiopulmonary patterns—no wearables required. Residents don't wear anything, don't charge anything, and don't even know they're being monitored.

AI models convert this data into predictive insights and automated alerts, empowering staff to act before deterioration occurs. It's like giving your nursing team a sixth sense for spotting trouble before it happens.

Seamless Integration. Intelligent Insights.

EMR Compatible

Integrates with PointClickCare, eClinicalWorks, Athena, and more. Data flows directly into your existing workflow.

Fully Encrypted

HIPAA-compliant with data encrypted in transit and at rest. Complete audit logs and access controls.

Real-Time Dashboards

Live insights for staff and clinicians. Monitor entire facilities or individual residents.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Machine learning models learn each resident's baseline and predict declines before they happen.

Easy Installation. Zero Disruption.

Our team handles everything from start to finish. Sensors install in minutes without any construction or wiring. Staff training takes less than an hour.

Within days, your facility is fully operational with continuous monitoring in place. Residents experience no disruption to their daily routines.

And because everything integrates with your existing EMR, there's no learning curve for documentation. It just works.
