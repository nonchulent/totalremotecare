Why We Built This

Walk into any long-term care facility at 3 AM, and you'll find dedicated nurses moving room to room, checking vitals, documenting by hand, wondering: "What am I missing?"

We saw skilled professionals spending more time on paperwork than patient care. Early warning signs slipping through because no one can be everywhere at once. Data scattered across clipboards—only becoming clear after residents were already in crisis.

We built technology that continuously monitors, intelligently analyzes, and proactively alerts. Total Remote Care gives your team the ability to see patterns before they become problems, document automatically, and focus on what matters: caring for residents.

Questions We Hear Most

Here's what facility administrators typically want to know

How does continuous monitoring improve patient outcomes?
Our system tracks vital signs 24/7 and uses intelligent algorithms to detect subtle changes in patient health patterns. This early detection allows your staff to intervene before minor issues become serious complications, reducing emergency situations and hospital transfers. Residents receive timely, proactive care based on real data rather than scheduled check-ins.
Will this actually save our nurses time, or add more work?
It saves significant time. Automated vital sign documentation eliminates manual charting, data flows directly into your EHR without duplicate entry, and smart alerts reduce interruptions by filtering out false alarms. Most facilities report nurses save 2+ hours per shift on documentation alone—time they can spend on direct patient care.
How quickly can residents benefit from the monitoring?
Most facilities see benefits within the first week. After initial setup and staff training (typically 3-5 days), the system begins learning baseline patterns for each resident. Within 7-10 days, our algorithms can accurately detect meaningful changes and alert your team to potential issues before they escalate.
Does this integrate with our existing EHR system?
Yes. We integrate with major long-term care EHR systems including PointClickCare, MatrixCare, and others. Vital sign data flows directly into resident charts without duplicate entry, and all documentation meets CMS requirements. We handle the technical integration during setup.
How do you ensure resident privacy and data security?
All systems are HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption. We conduct regular security audits and provide staff training on privacy protocols. Only authorized personnel have access to resident data, and all access is logged for compliance. Your facility maintains complete control over who can view patient information.
