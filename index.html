How We Support Your Nursing Home

Addressing the daily challenges your facility faces with comprehensive solutions

How do we help nursing staff manage night shift emergencies more effectively?
Our 24/7 monitoring system acts as an extra set of eyes during night shifts when staffing is minimal. We provide instant alerts for critical changes in resident vitals, automated fall detection, and direct access to on-call physicians. This means your night staff can respond faster to emergencies and have expert backup when making critical decisions, reducing stress and improving outcomes.
What specific support do we provide for dementia and memory care residents?
We offer specialized monitoring protocols for memory care units including wandering prevention alerts, sleep pattern analysis, and behavioral tracking. Our system identifies changes in routine that may indicate progression or distress, helping staff intervene early. We also provide tools for documenting behaviors and interventions, making it easier to identify what works best for each resident.
How does our system help reduce staff burnout and overtime hours?
By automating routine vital collection and documentation, we free up 2-3 hours per shift that nurses can dedicate to direct resident care. Our predictive alerts help prevent crisis situations that require all-hands responses. The system also streamlines shift handoffs with automated reports, reducing the time spent in meetings and allowing staff to leave on time.
How do we help prevent falls and other common nursing home incidents?
Our AI analyzes patterns to identify residents at high risk for falls based on mobility changes, medication effects, and time-of-day factors. We provide real-time alerts when at-risk residents attempt to stand unassisted, and our bed and chair sensors detect unusual movement patterns. This proactive approach has helped facilities reduce fall rates by up to 40% within the first six months.
What tools do we provide to improve family communication and satisfaction?
Families receive secure access to view their loved one's health trends, care activities, and progress notes through our family portal. Automated updates keep families informed of significant changes or milestones. During care conferences, our comprehensive reports with easy-to-understand visualizations help families feel more involved and confident in the care being provided, leading to higher satisfaction scores.
