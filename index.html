Why We Built This

Walk into any long-term care facility at 3 AM, and you'll find dedicated nurses moving room to room, checking vitals, documenting by hand, and wondering: "What am I missing?"

We saw skilled professionals spending more time on paperwork than patient care. Early warning signs slipping through the cracks because no one can be everywhere at once. Data scattered across clipboards—heart rates, oxygen levels, sleep patterns—only becoming obvious after residents were already in crisis.

There had to be a better way. Technology that continuously monitors, intelligently analyzes, and proactively alerts. A system that empowers nursing staff with insights when they need them, not hours later when reviewing charts.

Total Remote Care gives your team the ability to see patterns before they become problems, document automatically, and focus on what matters most: caring for residents.

— Founder & CEO, Total Remote Care

Supporting Your Facility

Solutions for your daily challenges

How does this help with night shift staffing?
Our 24/7 monitoring system acts as extra support during night shifts. We provide instant alerts for critical changes, automated fall detection, and direct access to on-call physicians, helping your staff respond faster and make better decisions.
What about memory care residents?
We offer specialized monitoring including wandering prevention alerts, sleep pattern analysis, and behavioral tracking. Our system identifies changes that may indicate distress, helping staff intervene early and effectively.
How much staff time will this save?
By automating routine vital collection and documentation, we free up significant time per shift. Our predictive alerts prevent crisis situations, and streamlined shift handoffs reduce meeting time, allowing staff to focus on direct care.
Can this really prevent falls?
Yes. Our AI analyzes patterns to identify high-risk residents based on mobility changes and medications. Real-time alerts notify staff when at-risk residents attempt to stand unassisted, significantly reducing fall incidents.
How do families stay informed?
Families receive secure access to health trends and care activities through our portal. Automated updates keep them informed of changes, and our visual reports help them feel more involved and confident in the care provided.
What's the implementation timeline?
Full implementation takes just 2-4 weeks. We handle device setup, EHR integration, and comprehensive staff training. Your facility can start seeing improvements in care quality and efficiency within the first month.
